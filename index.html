Major Projects

Relay

Relay is a user-friendly web application that bridges the gap between exercise and safety. The goal of Relay is to provide peace of mind to individuals who are exercising outdoors by keeping their selected contact apprised of their location without distracting them from their workout. The app also has features to send selected check-ins, notifications for the start and finish of their exercise, and quick access to direct call contact and open their camera.

Technologies Used: React.js, Passport.js, bcrypt, Mongoose, Express.js, Vonage API, and Material-UI

Esthete Gallery

Esthete Gallery is an art gallery management system that allows an art gallery owner to maintain details of artists and paintings with great ease. With the help of this app, gallery owners can store, view, and maintain inventory. In addition to maintenance, the app also allows owner to post future events and keep customers/users upto date on exhibitions being held by the Gallery.

Technologies Used: MySQL, Sequelize, Passport.js, bycryptjs, cloudinary, dotenv, and express

Moodii

The beginning of our course corresponded with the beginning of the pandemic. During this time, many people were at home, getting stir crazy and unsure what to do with their time. There were several reports at that time concerning the effects of the pandemic and society's mental health. Drawing inspiration from this crisis, we decided to create an app that is a digital mood ring. When the user opens the app, they input their current mood, and be provided with a selection of movies to help support their mood.

Technologies Used: The Movie Database API, Bulma, Google Fonts, and JQuery
